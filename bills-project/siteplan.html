Site Plan | Website Content

Site Name

Household Bills

This name represents the objective of the website that is to help people with Household bills

---------------------------------------------

Site Purpose

To help people organize their household bills by keeping a register and choosing to economize or stay spending as they use to.

---------------------------------------------

Scenarios

Question: Which are the three mais bills people need to pay?

Answer: Electricity, water and gas

Question: How could people save money by controlling their bills?

Answer: By knowing the medium value they have spend in the last months and choosing to keep or to make an economy, setting the value of objective and receiving tips that explain what can be done.

---------------------------------------------
